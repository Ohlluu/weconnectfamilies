Our Story

Connecting Families Since 2014

We Connect Families was born from a simple belief: that maintaining family bonds during incarceration is crucial for rehabilitation and community healing. For over 10 years, we've provided reliable, affordable transportation to help families stay connected with their loved ones.

What started as weekend trips to a few facilities has grown into a comprehensive service covering 19 correctional facilities across New York State. We understand the challenges families face, and we're here to make visits easier and more accessible.
