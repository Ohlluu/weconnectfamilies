About WE Connect Families

Since 2014, WE Connect Families has been the trusted transportation service connecting families with their incarcerated loved ones across New York State. WE understand that maintaining these important relationships shouldn't be limited by distance or transportation barriers.

Our experienced team provides safe, reliable, and dignified transportation services every weekend to 18 correctional facilities throughout New York. We've helped thousands of families stay connected during some of life's most challenging times.
